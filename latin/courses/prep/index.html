Study Tips

This course introduces Latin through stories about Roman life. Don't worry about memorising everything - focus on understanding the stories first!

Practise reading the Latin aloud to get familiar with the sounds.

About Suburani

Suburani means "people from the Subura" - Rome's bustling district. Follow Sabina and her family through adventures in ancient Rome and Britain!
