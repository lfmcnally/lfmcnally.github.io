Section 1 Translation: Messalina was inflamed with a new and almost insane love. For she had burned with love for C. Silius, the most handsome of the Roman youth, to such an extent that she drove Junia Silana, a noble woman, from her marriage, and got possession of an unrestricted adulterer.
