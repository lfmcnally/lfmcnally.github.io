Look, Cover, Write, Check

This is a classic vocabulary learning method, ideal for building strong recall of Latin words.

LOOK at the word list and try to memorise the translations. Take as long as you need.

COVER hides the English meanings. You can no longer see them!

WRITE your translations from memory. Type what you think each word means.

CHECK your answers against the correct translations. Green means correct, red means try again.

Each round your previous answers are kept so you can see your progress and which words you keep getting wrong.

Chunking: Start with a small group of words and unlock more as you gain confidence. This stops you feeling overwhelmed!
