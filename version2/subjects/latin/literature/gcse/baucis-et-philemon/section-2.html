Content — what is happening
  • Reaching the little cottage, the gods bow their heads to pass beneath its low doorway, and old Philemon invites them to rest on a couch spread with a rough homespun cloth.
  • Busy Baucis rakes the warm ash, revives yesterday’s embers with leaves and dry bark, and coaxes a flame with her old woman’s breath.
  • She brings split kindling and dry sticks down from the roof, breaks them up, and sets a small bronze pot over the fire.
  • Philemon fetches cabbage from the well-watered garden and cuts a thin slice from a long-kept side of bacon to soften in the boiling water — the couple give the best of their little store.
