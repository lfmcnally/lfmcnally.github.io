Content — what is happening
  • The couple while away the intervening hours with conversation so that the gods do not feel the delay.
  • A beechwood basin, hung from a nail by a sturdy handle, is filled with warm water to bathe the guests’ weary limbs.
  • A couch with a willow frame and a soft sedge mattress is made up and spread with a coverlet kept only for holidays — cheap and old, yet no disgrace to such a couch.
  • The gods take their places and recline.
