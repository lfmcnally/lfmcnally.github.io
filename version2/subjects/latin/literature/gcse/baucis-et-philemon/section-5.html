Content — what is happening
  • Both old people obey the gods and, leaning on their staffs, struggle up the long slope, almost reaching the summit.
  • Looking back, they see that every other house has been submerged in a marsh — only their own cottage has been spared.
  • While they marvel at this and weep for the fate of their neighbours, the humble cottage is transformed before their eyes into a golden temple, with marble columns, a gilded roof, carved doors and a marble floor.
  • Jupiter (the son of Saturn) then speaks calmly, praising the just couple and inviting them to ask for whatever they wish; Philemon, after a brief word with Baucis, declares their shared decision to the gods.
