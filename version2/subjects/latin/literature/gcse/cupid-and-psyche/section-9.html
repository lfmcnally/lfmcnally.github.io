Content — what is happening
  • When the shepherd-god has spoken and gone — Psyche answering nothing, only worshipping the deity who saved her — she sets off on her way.
  • After trudging a long stretch of road on weary feet, lost on some path as the day fades, she comes to a city ruled by the husband of one of her sisters.
  • Learning this, she asks to be announced; soon she is led in, the greetings and embraces are exchanged, and when her sister asks why she has come, she begins:
  • ‘You remember, of course, the advice by which you urged me to kill with a two-edged razor the beast who, under the false name of husband, slept beside me — before his ravenous throat could swallow poor little me.’
