The Odyssey

The compulsory epic, read in the order the exam needs it understood. Prescribed books: 1, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 16, 17, 18, 19, 21, 22 and 23. Each topic pairs its books with a lead theme, and the unprescribed books are covered in summary along the way. Lessons are being added topic by topic.
