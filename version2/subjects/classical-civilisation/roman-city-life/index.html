Roman City Life

Two halves: the Culture of the Roman city (Section A) — housing, the home and family, society and leisure — and the Literature of Roman satire and letters (Section B): Horace, Juvenal, Petronius and Pliny. The six prescribed inscriptions (Cooley & Cooley) are woven through the Society and Leisure lessons as documentary evidence. Notes, source and extract analysis, exam focus and flashcards are being added lesson by lesson.
