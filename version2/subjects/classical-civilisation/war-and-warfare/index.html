War and Warfare

Two halves: the Culture of ancient warfare (Section A) — Sparta and Athens in the fifth century, and the Roman military and its campaigns — and the Literature of war (Section B): Homer's Iliad, Tyrtaeus, Horace and Virgil. Each lesson focuses on a source, site, set passage or theme — notes, source and extract analysis, exam focus and flashcards are being added lesson by lesson.
