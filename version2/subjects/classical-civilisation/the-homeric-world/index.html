The Homeric World

Two halves: the Culture of the Mycenaean age (Section A) — key sites, daily life, decorative arts and burial — and the Literature of Homer's Odyssey (Section B), Books 9, 10, 19, 21 and 22. Each lesson focuses on a site, source, book or theme — notes, source and extract analysis, exam focus and flashcards are being added lesson by lesson.
