How to play

Latin Quest

🗣️ Follow the story. The golden beam of light and the banner at the top always show where to go next. A gold ! marks the person who needs you.

📖 Discover the world's words. Walk up to the well, the river, the temple, the animals — their Latin names appear, and first finds go into your Lexicon for bonus XP.

🐑 Help the townsfolk. Fetch what Livia asks for (read the crate labels carefully!), win the runaway animals over with a basket of feed and lead the parade home, chase windblown pages, and dig where a Latin scroll says the lost cargo fell.

🌙 Find the Sibyl. When the town trusts you, the Oracle will tell you what the way home will cost…

💾 Your progress is saved on this device automatically — and if you're signed in to Classicalia, every answer feeds the same smart tracking as the vocabulary tester, so your teacher can see your progress.
