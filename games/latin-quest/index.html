How to play

Latin Quest

🗣️ Talk to the townsfolk. A gold ! above someone's head means they have a challenge for you. Each person tests one chapter of the OCR GCSE vocabulary list.

🏆 Earn laurels. Answer 8 questions correctly to win a laurel. Missed words come back around until you get them right — and your accuracy decides your stars, so you can return any time to improve them.

🔮 Face the Sibyl. Once you hold all ten laurels, the Sibyl in the temple will set you a final trial drawn from the whole word list.

💾 Your progress is saved on this device automatically.
