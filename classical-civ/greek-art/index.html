Study Tips

Focus on understanding the development from Archaic stylisation to Classical naturalism - this progression is fundamental to understanding Greek art.

Learn to describe works precisely: pose, drapery, facial expression, composition. The exam expects detailed visual analysis.

Prescribed Sources

All lessons include high-quality images of prescribed sources from museum collections worldwide.

Key collections: British Museum, Louvre, National Archaeological Museum Athens, Vatican Museums.
