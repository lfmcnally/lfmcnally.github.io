About 10-Markers

10-mark questions are passage-based. You'll receive an extract from one of the prescribed books and a question asking you to analyse a specific aspect.

Common question types include:

  • How does Homer make X vivid?
  • How is X characterised?
  • How does Homer create tension/emotion?

Study Tips

Focus on close textual analysis. Use specific details from the passage and Homer's literary techniques.

Aim for 3-4 well-developed points with quotations and analysis.
