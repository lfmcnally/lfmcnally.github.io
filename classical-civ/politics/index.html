Study Tips

Start with Topic 1 to understand Republican structures before exploring how they collapsed.

Focus on key individuals and their motivations - politics was deeply personal in Rome.

About This Course

This course charts the dramatic century that saw the Roman Republic tear itself apart through civil war and political violence.

From the Gracchi to Caesar's assassination, explore how ambition, ideology, and constitutional crisis destroyed Rome's greatest political achievement.
