About This Lesson

This lesson covers the essential pronunciation marks in Ancient Greek:

  • Breathing marks - tell you whether to add an 'h' sound
  • Accent marks - show which syllable to stress
  • Diphthongs - vowel combinations that make single sounds
  • Iota subscript - a special mark under certain vowels

Master these basics and reading Greek becomes much easier!
